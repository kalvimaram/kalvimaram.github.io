Our Initiatives

What we do

Safe drinking water, Clean toilets
A basic need for government schools

Kalvi Maram Foundation's 'Nalamudan Namadhu Palli' (நலமுடன் நமது பள்ளி) initiative ensures government school health by installing and managing RO water purifiers, monthly toilet cleaning supplies, and hygiene education for students. From an initial pilot in 3 schools, we have now expanded to 15 government schools (2025-26), ensuring every child learns with dignity.

Safe Drinking Water

We have installed and maintain RO water purifiers across 15 government schools, providing 2,475 students with consistent access to safe, clean drinking water. Students who have access to safe drinking water have greater clarity and focus, allowing them to think and absorb lessons more quickly while improving their concentration levels.

Learn More

Toilet Cleaning Supplies

We provide monthly toilet cleaning supplies to selected rural government schools in Tamil Nadu to ensure proper hygiene and reduce health risks among students. Students in government schools are more likely to be diagnosed with infections of the urinary tract, bladder, and kidney due to poor toilet hygiene.

Learn More

Tree Planting in School Campuses

Kalvi Maram Foundation promotes tree planting in government school campuses, aiming to foster environmental awareness and biodiversity among students. By involving children in planting and caring for trees, we create green spaces that improve air quality and instill ecological responsibility.

Learn More

FREE TNPSC Training

We provide free training and guidance for government exams, with a concentration on TNPSC exams. Our comprehensive training programs help rural students prepare for competitive examinations and secure government jobs.

Learn More

Career Guidance

Career Guidance is a counselling programme that assists students in determining the best career path for them based on their educational and professional choices. Kalvi Maram Foundation provides free career counselling to help students make informed decisions about their future.

Learn More
