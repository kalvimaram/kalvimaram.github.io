Support Government Schools

Students in government schools are more likely to be diagnosed with infections of the urinary tract, bladder, and kidney, as well as failure to maintain toilet hygiene at school.

Clean Drinking Water

Students who have access to safe drinking water have greater clarity and focus, allowing them to think and absorb lessons more quickly while improving their concentration levels. To ensure safety in storage and handling, a drinking water dispenser system should be installed.

FREE TNPSC Training

We provide free training and guidance for government exams, with a concentration on TNPSC exams.

Career Guidance

Career Guidance is a counselling programme that assists students in determining the best career path for them based on their educational and professional choices. Which career options are available are determined by a qualitative and quantitative assessment of the student's knowledge, skills, information, and experience.

Learn More

Mini Forests

Trees are essential to the health and well-being of our planet and play a crucial role in maintaining a healthy and balanced ecosystem. Protecting and planting trees is a critical step towards creating a sustainable future for all. We are teaching students about the importance of trees and establishing mini forests for birds.
