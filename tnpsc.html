🎯Free Online Coaching Initiative for TNPSC & TNUSRB Exams

💧Our Mission

Driven by its core mission to uplift underserved students across Tamil Nadu, the foundation has launched a free online coaching program targeting three pivotal government recruitment exams: TNPSC Group 4, TNUSRB Sub-Inspector, and TNUSRB Police Constable.

🌟 Why Safe Water Matters

  • Protecting Student Health: Contaminated water can cause dangerous illnesses. Our initiative prevents this by ensuring every sip is pure and safe.
  • Empowering Education: Healthy children learn better. They concentrate more, miss fewer school days, and grow into confident learners.
  • Promoting Equity: Students from all backgrounds deserve safe water. This program removes barriers so every child gets the same healthy start.

🔧 What We Do

  • Install high-quality Reverse Osmosis (RO) water purifiers in schools
  • Regularly maintain systems and check water quality to ensure consistent safety
  • Educate students and staff about clean water practices
  • Monitor and evaluate the impact of our water safety programs
  • Collaborate with school authorities for sustainable implementation

💙 Our Impact

🏫 Schools Transformed
  • 15 government schools equipped with RO water purifiers
  • 2,475 students now have consistent access to safe drinking water
  • Regular maintenance and monitoring ensures water quality
  • Reduced water-borne illnesses among students
👧🏽👦🏽 Students Empowered
  • Improved attendance rates due to better health
  • Enhanced concentration and learning outcomes
  • Increased awareness about water hygiene
  • Every child can drink water with confidence

🎯 Our Achievement: Kalvi Maram Foundation has successfully installed and maintains RO water purifiers across 15 government schools, providing 2,475 students with consistent access to safe, clean drinking water.

💙 Conclusion

'நலமுடன் நமது பள்ளி' is not just a program—it's a movement for dignity, health, and learning. Safe drinking water in schools builds not only stronger students, but a stronger Tamil Nadu.

Every drop of clean water is a step towards a healthier, more educated future for our children.
