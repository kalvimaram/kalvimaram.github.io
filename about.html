About Us

Welcome to Kalvi Maram Foundation

Kalvi Maram Foundation is a non-profit organisation that guides rural students to higher education, provides financial assistance to poor students, assists with basic needs in government schools, raises awareness about the importance of clean drinking water, toilet hygiene, and trees, and works to establish mini forests.

Kalvi Maram Foundation's projects are all focused on Education, Health, and a Better Society. Education is a powerful instrument for transforming the lives of underprivileged children.
