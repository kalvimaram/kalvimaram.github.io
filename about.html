About Us

Empowering Education Through Health & Hygiene

Kalvi Maram Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to transforming government schools into nurturing spaces where every child can thrive. Founded by passionate alumni of Government schools, we bridge the critical gap between education and essential health infrastructure.

Our work addresses a fundamental truth: a child's learning potential is deeply connected to their school environment. While education policies focus on curriculum and teaching, we champion the often-overlooked foundations of effective learning—clean water, proper sanitation, and health awareness.

  • Alumni-Led Insight: Our team intimately understands government school challenges
  • Sustainable Model: Local partnerships ensure long-term maintenance
  • Measurable Impact: Every project tracks weekly status updates, issues and improvements
